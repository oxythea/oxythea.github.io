About

Oxythea is
the breath
of the future

Oxythea was created by SmartCon AI to fight air pollution in Armenia and around the world. Our artificial intelligence analyzes environmental data, coordinates, and terrain characteristics, then builds precise tree planting programs.

We believe technology and nature can work together to create a healthier, cleaner, and greener world.

Product

SmartCon AI —
what it creates

Our platform creates comprehensive environmental solutions by combining satellite data, climate analysis, and tree planting strategies.

🌫️

Air pollution

We analyze fine dust, carbon dioxide, and nitrogen oxide levels across the territory in real time.

🗺️

Coordinate analysis

With accurate geolocation, we study each area and assess soil quality and climate.

🌳

Tree planting program

The system calculates which tree to plant where to provide maximum oxygen, shade, and resilience.

📋

Action plan

We create concrete, actionable programs for municipalities, businesses, and volunteer organizations.

📡

Continuous monitoring

Complete tracking of planted tree growth, environmental impact, and air quality improvement.

📊

Reports

Complete air quality reports for government, NGOs, investors, and the public.
